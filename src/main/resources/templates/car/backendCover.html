WELCOME TO MY CAR SALES APPLICATION

This is a car sales application using spring MVC, data JPA, ThymeLeaf, Security, java, html, CSS, javascript, boostrap and Mysql database. It showcases ways to do the CRUD based operations(create, save, delete and updating) my different models as well as showing their relationships.
