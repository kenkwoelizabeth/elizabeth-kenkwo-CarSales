About Us

Lizzy Autos is a leading digital marketplace and solutions provider for the
automotive industry that sell nice and affordable cars. Launched in 1998
headquartered in Georgia, the Company empowers shoppers with the data,
resources and digital tools needed to make informed buying decisions and
seamlessly make purchases. In a rapidly changing market, Lizzy Autos
enables dealerships and OEMs with innovative technical solutions and data-
driven intelligence to better reach and influence ready-to-buy shoppers,
increase inventory turn and gain market share.
